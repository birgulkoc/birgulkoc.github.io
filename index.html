Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am working in numerical analysis and scientific computing with a particular emphasis on numerical methods for fluid flows. My research interests include reduced order model (ROM) development, closure modeling, data-driven (DD) modeling, variational multiscale (VMS) methods, numerical analysis of ROM closures, and proper orthogonal decomposition (POD).

Currently, I am working on transport and porous media problems as a postdoctoral researcher at IFPEN & INRIA MEMPHIS in France.
